NCRian ecosystem

Since NCR offers banking, retails, grocery, resturants and many other information technology solutions, I decided to taking the advantage of its large influnce to build NCR ecosystem that makes all clients and users linked and connected.

This feature allows NCR cliens and users to make transctions online basing on the clients' product and service provided with users' need where also a client can be a user to other client.

Such as: A resturant owner client A would like to purchase supplies from a grocery store client B. This feature would do the online transcation.

Such as: A grocery store client A has voucher and promotion to its local business owners or publics. In this feature, sellers would post the infomation, and buyer can browse and make purchase.

Report and state inspection

For business owners, they would be able to see the analytic reports and take reaction.

From the report, owners can catch the peak serving time when it's busy to maximize labor utilization or reduce labor cost when it's slow.

Such as: A resturant or hotel owner client A provides its service near Mercedes-Benz Stadium whereas hosting a NFL game. This feature would help to scale.

Such as: A coffee shop client A opens when the weather is horrible. This feature would help to notify.
